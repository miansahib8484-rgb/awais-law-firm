    SEO Writing Rules

    Follow these every article for Google to rank you

    01
    Title: 50–60 characters. Put the main keyword near the start.
    02
    Meta Description: 140–160 characters. Include keyword + a reason to click.
    03
    Focus Keyword: Must appear in title, meta description, and body naturally.
    04
    Body: Minimum 300 words. Use ## headings every 2–3 paragraphs.
    05
    Tags: 3–8 comma-separated terms. Think what your clients search for.
    06
    Image: Always upload a photo. Articles with images get far more Google clicks.

    Google Search Console Steps

    Do this after every article you publish

    1
    Publish your article here and copy the article link shown in the preview.
    2
    Go to search.google.com/search-console and sign in with the site's Google account.
    3
    Click "URL Inspection" in the left sidebar.
    4
    Paste your article link in the search bar at the top and press Enter.
    5
    Click "Request Indexing". Google will crawl and add your article to search results.
    6
    Results appear on Google within 24–72 hours for new articles.
