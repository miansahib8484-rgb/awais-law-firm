Who We
Are.

Awais Law Associates (ALA) is a dynamic, forward-thinking law firm founded by Muhammad Awais Ahsan Joiya — Former Assistant Advocate General, Punjab, and practicing Advocate of the High Courts of Pakistan.

The firm represents corporates, financial institutions, overseas Pakistanis, and private clients in complex litigation and advisory matters. ALA combines meticulous legal research, rigorous drafting capability, and articulate advocacy — developed across two decades at Pakistan's most prestigious courts.

Awais Law Associates is committed to the highest professional and ethical standards. A formal Code of Professional and Ethical Policy governs every engagement — ensuring every client receives candid, responsible, and trustworthy counsel.

The firm also recognises its Corporate Social Responsibility. Pro bono services are rendered to less-privileged citizens, and ALA provides a platform for young law graduates to develop skills in legal research and advocacy.
