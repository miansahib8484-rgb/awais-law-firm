Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a case take?

The timeline depends on the complexity of the matter and court schedules. We prioritise efficiency without compromising legal integrity. Simple matters may resolve in weeks; complex litigation can take months.

Do you offer initial consultations?

Yes. Consultations are available by appointment via our contact form or by calling directly. All matters are handled with strict confidentiality.

Do you handle overseas Pakistani matters?

Yes — ALA regularly advises overseas Pakistanis on property disputes, family law, succession, and cross-jurisdictional matters, including coordination with foreign courts.

What courts do you appear in?

We appear before the Supreme Court of Pakistan, Lahore High Court, and all subordinate courts and tribunals in Punjab, including NAB, banking, and family courts.
