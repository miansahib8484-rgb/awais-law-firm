Civil Litigation

Resolving property disputes, breach of contract claims, and complex civil matters through rigorous legal strategy and courtroom advocacy at every level of the Pakistani judicial system.

Property Disputes Contract Claims Injunctions Recovery Suits Declaratory Decrees
Book Consultation

Civil litigation forms the cornerstone of ALA's practice. The firm has represented individuals, corporations, and government entities in a wide spectrum of civil disputes — from straightforward property boundary claims to multi-party, multi-jurisdiction commercial disputes involving significant financial stakes.

The firm's approach to civil litigation is methodical and evidence-driven. Before any matter reaches court, ALA's legal team conducts a comprehensive pre-litigation analysis — reviewing title documents, contracts, correspondence, and financial records — to build the strongest possible foundation for the client's position.

ALA appears before civil courts at all levels including trial courts, the Lahore High Court (original and appellate jurisdiction).

Property & Land Disputes

Inheritance, ownership, boundary, encroachment, and illegal possession matters handled across Punjab. Full representation from revenue courts to the Lahore High Court.

Breach of Contract

Enforcement and defence of commercial, service, and employment contracts. Claims for damages, specific performance, and rescission before civil courts.

Recovery & Money Suits

Filing and defending suits for recovery of money, loans, security deposits, and contractual payments owed — including post-decree execution proceedings.

Injunctions & Stays

Urgent applications for temporary restraining orders, status quo injunctions, and stays of execution to protect clients' interests during pending litigation.

Declaratory Relief

Obtaining court declarations on rights, title, and legal status — particularly in matters of disputed ownership, family inheritance, or contractual ambiguity.

Appeals & Revisions

Filing and defending appeals, revisions, and second appeals before the High Court against civil court decrees and orders that are adverse to the client.

SECP & Regulatory Compliance

Constitutional & Writ Jurisdiction

Enforcing fundamental rights and challenging unlawful executive action through skilled constitutional advocacy before the all High Court of Pakistan.

Writ Petitions Fundamental Rights Public Interest Litigation Judicial Review Service Matters
Book Consultation

Constitutional law sits at the heart of ALA's institutional identity. The founder participated directly — as a Senior Associate at Mandviwalla & Zafar — in some of the most consequential constitutional matters in Pakistan's recent legal history, including the PCO Judges Case, the LNG Case, Art 10-A right to fair trial proceedings, and the OGRA constitutional reference.

ALA files and argues writ petitions under Articles 199 In all High Courts Of Pakistan in matters involving fundamental rights violations, illegal detentions, unlawful government action, service disputes, and public interest matters.

The firm has a genuine commitment to public interest litigation — filing petitions on matters of national importance including the Kala Bagh Dam water resource case and contributing to legislative drafting on the Anti-Terrorism Act and Human Rights Commission Bill.

Fundamental Rights Petitions

Writ petitions before the LHC and Supreme Court challenging violations of Articles 9–28 — including rights to life, liberty, fair trial, equality, and freedom of expression.

Illegal Detention & Habeas Corpus

Urgent habeas corpus applications before the High Court for the immediate release of persons unlawfully detained or held without legal justification.

Service & Employment Matters

Constitutional challenges to wrongful dismissal, departmental action, promotion disputes, and seniority matters affecting civil servants and public sector employees.

Judicial Review of Government Action

Challenging unlawful administrative orders, illegal taxation, arbitrary exercise of regulatory power, and government contracts that violate constitutional rights.

Public Interest Litigation

Filing petitions on matters of public concern — environment, water resources, governance failures, and systemic rights violations — before the all Courts of Pakistan under Article 184(3).

Constitutional Advisory

Advisory opinions on the constitutional compliance of proposed legislation, government policies, and regulatory frameworks — for NGOs, businesses, and public bodies.

NAB & FIA Defence

Defending individuals and organisations facing criminal charges, NAB accountability proceedings, FIA investigations, and anti-corruption inquiries — with discretion, strategy, and robust advocacy.

NAB Defence FIA Investigations Bail Applications Criminal Appeals Anti-Terrorism Act White-Collar Crime
Book Consultation

Proceedings before the National Accountability Bureau (NAB) and the Federal Investigation Agency (FIA) often involve complex investigations related to white-collar crime, corruption, financial misconduct, cyber offences, and regulatory violations. Such matters require careful legal strategy, early intervention, and experienced representation.

ALA approaches NAB and FIA defence with seriousness and strategic depth, assisting clients from the earliest stages of investigation — including responding to NAB call-up notices, FIA summons, and inquiry notices — while ensuring the protection of their legal and constitutional rights.

The firm provides comprehensive representation throughout the process, from initial inquiries and investigations to proceedings before accountability courts and other competent forums, including bail applications, trial representation, and appellate challenges where required.

NAB Accountability Proceedings

Defence in NAB inquiries, investigations, and references before Accountability Courts — from initial call-up notices through trial and appeals before the High Court.

FIA & Anti-Corruption Cases

Representation in FIA cybercrime, foreign exchange, anti-corruption, and human trafficking investigations, including responses to FIA show-cause notices and summons.

Bail Applications

Urgent bail applications at trial court and High Court level — including pre-arrest bail (anticipatory bail) and post-arrest bail in NAB, FIA, and ordinary criminal matters.

NAB Proceedings Defence

Representation in inquiries and investigations conducted by the National Accountability Bureau, including responses to call-up notices, arrest and bail matters, and defence before Accountability Courts.

FIA Investigations & Proceedings

Legal assistance in matters investigated by the Federal Investigation Agency, including white-collar crime, financial offences, and cybercrime cases — from responding to FIA summons through trial and appellate proceedings.

White-Collar & Regulatory Crime

Defence in financial crime matters — including tax evasion, securities fraud, money laundering (AMLA), and corporate compliance failures with criminal consequences.

Tax & Revenue Law

Navigating Pakistan's complex tax framework — challenging unlawful assessments, advising on compliance obligations, and representing clients before revenue authorities and appellate forums.

Income Tax Sales Tax / GST FBR Appeals Customs & Duties Tax Planning Withholding Tax
Book Consultation

Pakistan's tax system — administered by the Federal Board of Revenue and provincial revenue authorities — presents significant complexity for businesses and individuals alike. Tax assessments can be arbitrary, tax demands disproportionate, and the appeal process multi-layered and procedurally demanding.

The firm represents clients before FBR officers, Commissioners of Inland Revenue, Appellate Tribunals, and — where constitutional challenges to tax legislation are involved — the all Courts of Pakistan.

Income Tax Disputes

Challenging arbitrary income tax assessments, best-of-judgment orders, and additional tax demands — at CIR level, ATIR, and constitutional challenges before the High Court.

Sales Tax & GST

Advisory and dispute resolution on Federal and provincial sales tax — including input tax adjustments, exemptions, registration requirements, and penalty challenges.

Customs & Import Duties

Representing importers and exporters in customs duty disputes, valuation challenges, anti-dumping proceedings, and seizure or confiscation cases.

Tax Appeals & Tribunal Proceedings

Filing and arguing appeals before the Commissioner (Appeals), Appellate Tribunal Inland Revenue (ATIR), and Federal Tax Ombudsman on behalf of individual and corporate taxpayers.

Tax Planning & Structuring

Advising on tax-efficient structuring for M&A transactions, real estate investments, business setups, and succession planning within applicable tax legislation.

FBR Investigation Defence

Representing clients subject to FBR tax audits, monitoring investigations, and prosecutions under the Income Tax Ordinance 2001 and Sales Tax Act 1990.

Trademark & Intellectual Property

Protecting your brand, innovations, and creative works through trademark registration, IP enforcement, and litigation — securing the intellectual assets that define your business.

Trademark Registration Patent Advisory Copyright Protection IP Litigation Brand Protection Infringement Actions
Book Consultation

In an increasingly competitive marketplace, intellectual property rights are among a business's most valuable — and most vulnerable — assets. ALA provides comprehensive intellectual property legal services in Pakistan, from the initial registration of trademarks and patents through the enforcement of rights against infringers in civil and criminal courts.

The firm advises clients on the full scope of IP protection available under Pakistani law — including the Trade Marks Ordinance, Patents Ordinance, Copyright Ordinance, and the Registered Designs Ordinance — and provides strategic counsel on building and maintaining a strong IP portfolio.

Where IP rights are infringed, ALA moves quickly — seeking Anton Piller orders, injunctions, and damages before the civil courts, and pursuing criminal prosecution where appropriate.

Trademark Registration & Portfolio

Filing, prosecuting, and managing trademark applications at the Intellectual Property Organisation (IPO) Pakistan — including opposition proceedings and renewal management.

Patent & Design Advisory

Advisory on patentability, filing of patent applications at IPO Pakistan, and prosecution of patent applications — with coordination for international protection under PCT.

Copyright Registration & Protection

Registration of copyright in literary, artistic, musical, and software works — and advising on moral rights, fair dealing exceptions, and licensing of copyright-protected content.

IP Infringement Litigation

Civil suits for trademark passing off, copyright infringement, and patent infringement — including urgent injunction applications to stop infringing activity immediately.

IP Licensing & Agreements

Drafting and negotiating trademark licensing, franchise, technology transfer, and co-existence agreements — ensuring client IP rights are protected in all commercial arrangements.

Brand Protection Strategy

Developing comprehensive brand protection strategies — including monitoring programmes, customs recordal, and enforcement protocols — to proactively defend client IP assets.

Arbitration & Dispute Resolution

Representing parties in domestic and international arbitration — including proceedings before the London Court of International Arbitration — with the analytical depth and advocacy skills that complex commercial disputes demand.

LCIA Arbitration Domestic Arbitration Arbitration Agreement Drafting Award Enforcement Mediation
Book Consultation

Arbitration offers significant advantages over court litigation for certain commercial disputes — particularly those involving international parties, complex technical matters, or where confidentiality is critical. ALA has direct experience in international arbitration proceedings, having assisted in LCIA arbitrations — including Descon Engineering Ltd. v. Halmore Power Generation Co. and Imran Mumtaz v. Pakistan Public Works Dept.

This international arbitration experience, combined with a thorough understanding of the Arbitration Act 1940 and the Recognition and Enforcement (Arbitration Agreements and Foreign Arbitral Awards) Act 2011, enables ALA to advise clients on all aspects of the arbitral process.

ALA also represents clients in mediation, negotiated dispute resolution, and other forms of alternative dispute resolution — providing commercially realistic advice on when litigation, arbitration, or negotiated settlement best serves the client's interests.

Arbitration Clause Drafting

Drafting and reviewing arbitration clauses in commercial contracts — specifying seat, governing law, number of arbitrators, rules, and language to ensure a workable and enforceable arbitration agreement.

Foreign Award Enforcement

Enforcement of foreign arbitral awards in Pakistan under the New York Convention and the Recognition and Enforcement Act 2011 — and challenging enforcement where grounds exist.

Court Referral & Stay Applications

Applying to stay court proceedings in favour of arbitration and compelling unwilling parties to arbitrate in accordance with their contractual agreement.

Mediation & Settlement

Representing clients in structured mediation and commercial negotiation — providing objective advice on settlement value and realistic assessment of litigation risk.

Our Approach

How We Handle Every Matter

A structured, client-focused process that ensures every engagement receives the same standard of research, strategy, and advocacy.

01

Initial Consultation

A confidential consultation to understand the client's situation in full — the facts, documents, objectives, and constraints — before any legal strategy is proposed. No assumptions, no shortcuts.

02

Legal Research & Analysis

Thorough research into applicable legislation, case law, and regulatory frameworks. ALA prepares a candid assessment of the legal merits, risks, and available courses of action — including alternatives to litigation where appropriate.

03

Strategy & Execution

A clear legal strategy is agreed with the client, with realistic timelines and expectations. The firm then executes — whether through drafting, negotiation, regulatory engagement, or court proceedings — with the same rigour at every stage.

04

Regular Communication

Clients are kept informed of all developments in their matter. ALA operates on a policy of candid, timely communication — clients are never left without an update or without access to their legal team when needed.
